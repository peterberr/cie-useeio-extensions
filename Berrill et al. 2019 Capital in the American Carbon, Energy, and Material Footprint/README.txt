This repository contains updated files associated with the journal article submission 
"Capital in the American Carbon, Energy, and Material Footprint".


Article Authors:

Peter Berrill1*, T. Reed Miller2, Yasushi Kondo3, Edgar G. Hertwich1,2
1 Center for Industrial Ecology, School of Forestry and Environmental Studies, Yale University, New Haven, USA
2 Department of Chemical and Environmental Engineering, Yale University, New Haven, USA
3 Faculty of Political Science and Economics, Waseda University, Tokyo, Japan
*Corresponding author

The files are consistent with the BEA's most recent (published 2018) benchmark Make and Use tables, with 405 sectors. 
We add three sectors to that resolution to cover houshold domestic and transport emissions. 
The scripts are written in Matlab and the data files in .mat format can be opened in Matlab.
