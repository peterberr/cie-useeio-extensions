This directory contains the most recent version of Capital-Inclusive Footprint Tool, United States (CIFT-US).
The tool is intended for user to be able to calculate carbon, energy, and material footprints of products
and services consumed (purchased) in the US between 2005-2019.
Footprints can be calculated with and without the consideration of capital assets in the supply chain, enabling the
calculation of capital contributions to footprints of each product or service.

Instructional video can be found at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5LIctIVh5B0
